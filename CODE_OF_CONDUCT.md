# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We strive to build an inclusive, respectful, and professional community.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Assume positive intent.
- Welcome differing viewpoints.
- Communicate professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Hate speech
- Threats
- Deliberate disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in restricted participation.

Thank you for helping maintain a respectful community.
